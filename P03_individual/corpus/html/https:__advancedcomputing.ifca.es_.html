Advanced computing
and e-Science Group

Welcome to the Advanced Computing and e-Science group of the Physics Institute of Cantabria (IFCA), a joint centre with the combined effort of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC) and the University of Cantabria (UC) in Santander, Spain.

Our research efforts are focused on

Cloud Computing

Data Preservation

Data Science

Grid Computing

HPC Altamira Node

Our group was created in 2010 as a response to the growing scientific needs of powerful computing capacities and techniques. Since then, it has steadily expanded both its physical and human resources.
